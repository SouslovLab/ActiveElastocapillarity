Calculates \(g^{\rho \sigma} C_{\sigma}\) for particle i of the van Zuiden integrator.

This function calculates \(g^{\rho \sigma} C_{\sigma} = g^{\rho \sigma} (g^p_{\sigma \mu}(x^{\mu}-x^{\mu}_p)+h^2 \frac{F^{\sigma}}{m}) \) for particle i of the van Zuiden integrator for particle i. It overwrites ldf ZC[dim] after it is read as input.
