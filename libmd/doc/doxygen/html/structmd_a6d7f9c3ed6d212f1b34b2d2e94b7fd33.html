Calculate difference in particle velocities in certain dimension i by particle index.

Returns the dth component of the velocity difference between the closest periodic image of two particles indexed by p1 and p2. If the vector connecting the periodic images crosses a sheared boundary, the resulting velocity difference takes into account the shear velocity of the periodic image as well. The result is returned as a \(d\)-dimensional array of velocity components.
