Called after integration to keep the particle within the defined boundaries.

Update positions and velocities of particles to respect the appropriate boundary conditions.
This function loops through the dim dimensions, and updates the positions and velocities of all (non-fixed) particles that have passed through the boundary perpendicular to dimension d, based on the value of simbox.bcond[d].
