Manipulate particle distances with respect to periodic boundary conditions.

Given a displacement ad and corresponding spatial dimension d, calculates the displacement modulo the periodic box size in that dimension.

Under periodic boundary conditions, distances are required to lie in the range (-simbox.L[d]/2,simbox.L[d]/2). If the displacement dap is outside this range, multiples of simbox.L[d] are added or subtracted until the displacement is within these bounds, and the result is returned.
