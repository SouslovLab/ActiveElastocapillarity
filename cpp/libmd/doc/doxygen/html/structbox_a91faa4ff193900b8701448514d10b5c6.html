Skew the simulation box by moving boundary with normal direction j by amount 'displacement' in direction i.

Apply a static shear by displacing the boundary normal to spatial dimension j in direction i by an amount displacement. Requires \(i\neq j\). The boundary conditions in dimension j must be set to BOXSHEAR (set by default) or HARD; particle dynamics do not respect the shear if PERIODIC is used.
